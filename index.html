What is RimWOrld?

RimWorld is a sci-fi game that revolves around surviving in an alien planet. RimWorld is a construction, management and colony simulator.

The gameplay

RimWorld's main gamemode follows 3 survivors who crashlanded into an alien planet in the future. The survivors try to rebuild their lives; but during their time here, they will find other humans, animals, galatic federations, ancients and cults also live on this planet; some hostile and some not. As you rebuild, more humans and animals will join you and more will attack you too.
