2013

RimWorld started development in 2013 as “Eclipse colony,” before renaming to RimWorld in September of 2013. Tynan Sylvester, the lead developer of RimWorld greenlit a Kickstarter page in October of 2013. This Kickstarter would generate $290,000 from funding and over 9000 backers.

2018

After a tough 5 years of development, the game would sell a million copies in February of 2018 (before full release!) and would finally release on Steam on the 17th of November, 2018.

2020

The team released their first DLC called Royalty in 2020, adding a galatic empire faction to the game. By August 2020, it was estimated that RimWorld had sold over 100 million dollars in revenue, making RimWorld one of the most sold indie games ever on Steam.

2021

The team released their second DLC called Ideology in 2021, adding cults and religion to the game.

2022

The team released their third and latest DLC called Biotech in 2021, adding gene modification, children and robotic workers to the game.
